Fresh . Flavorful . Fusions

We offer Chinese noodles in two ways; the first is Chinese noodles prepared with oyster and soy sauce, and only vegetables can be added to it. Chicken, beef, or shrimp can also be added, cooked together in one dish. The second way is a dish of meat, chicken, or shrimp cooked with teriyaki and soy sauce, with vegetables added. It comes with either Chinese noodles or steamed rice on the side. Note that our main dish is prepared with fettuccine pasta, and for an additional 40 pounds, it can be turned into glass noodles, or for an additional 10 pounds, it can be turned into Korean noodles.
